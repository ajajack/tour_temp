CBDI Outpatient Tour

Proton Tour

Visit the Cincinnati Children’s side of the Proton Therapy Center where a gantry directs proton beam at a tumor, destroying cancer cells while minimizing damage to surrounding healthy tissue and organs.
Cyclotron Tour

Get a close-up view of the cyclotron, which uses water molecules to generate protons used to destroy cancer cells.
Research Proton Tour

Our Proton Therapy Center is the only center in the United States with a dedicated research gantry and is a national anchor for particle-based cancer research. Take a tour and see the equipment we’ll use to make advancements in the science and technique of proton therapy.
